Welcome to the DotA Match-Up Finder

DotA Match-Up Finder is a way to find high level public matches played with a specific hero match up. Find out how high level players deal with unfavorable match ups.

To get started, click the search icon on the top left. Or click the sign up button on the top right to save games you find to your favorites.
